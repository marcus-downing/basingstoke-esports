Basingstoke eSports

We arrange live showing of Overwatch League in a Basingstoke venue.

FAQ

Is there an entrance or membership fee?

No, there's no fee.

More questions...
Who can come? Are children allowed?

Anybody is welcome. We hope to create a friendly, welcoming environment.

Overwatch is rated 12A. Children under 12 should be accompanied by an adult.

Where is this happening?

The next event, on the 19th May, is at the The Bakers, Winchester Street, Basingstoke.

Map of the location and parking.

Where can I park?

There's parking directly behind The Bakers at Caston's Car Park or Albert Yard Car Park.

If you can't find a space there, there's paid parking very close at the Central Short Stay Car Park, or at Festival Place.

What time does it start?

The doors are open all day. We'll be showing Overwatch League from 6pm, and the live matches start at 9pm.

See the schedule below for more details.

Is there anywhere to eat nearby?

The pub have a menu and are happy to serve food directly to the room. There are plenty of restaurants and take-aways nearby, but please don't bring any food or drink from outside into the pub.

Will you do this every week?

If the first event is a success we'll plan more of them. So if you're interested, please do turn up and let us know.
